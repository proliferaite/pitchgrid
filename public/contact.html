What to Include in Your Message

To help us help you faster, please include:

  • Device info: Your phone model and Android version
  • App version: Check in the app's settings
  • Clear description: What you were trying to do and what happened
  • Steps to reproduce: If it's a bug, how can we recreate it?

Response Expectations

We're a small team, but we're committed to helping every user. Here's what to expect:

  • Bug reports: We prioritize these and usually respond within 24 hours
  • Feature requests: We'll acknowledge receipt and let you know our thoughts
  • General questions: We'll do our best to help or point you in the right direction
