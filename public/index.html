Key Features

🎥 Multi-Video Grid Analysis

Display up to 6 videos simultaneously in customizable grid layouts for comprehensive analysis of different angles and perspectives.

⚡ Advanced Synchronization

Precise video synchronization with custom pre-roll and post-roll settings, plus fixed-point sync for frame-perfect analysis.

✂️ Professional Cropping Tools

Preset aspect ratios and freeform cropping with drag-and-resize functionality for focused analysis of specific areas.

🔍 Zoom & Playback Controls

Pinch-to-zoom with persistent zoom states, slow motion playback, step-by-step frame analysis, and looping controls.

💾 Saved Configurations

Save and recall unlimited named layout presets for consistent analysis workflows across different sessions.

📤 Export & Share

Export analyzed videos with professional quality for sharing with athletes, teams, or for presentation purposes.
