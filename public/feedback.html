🌟 Submission Guidelines

  • Detailed Description: Explain your use case, bug, or feature request clearly and comprehensively
  • Visual Examples: Include screenshots, videos, or links to demonstrate your point
  • Step-by-Step Process: For use cases, explain how you set up your analysis
  • Results & Insights: Share what you discovered or achieved
  • Reproducible Steps: For bugs, provide clear steps to reproduce the issue
  • Device Information: Include your device model, Android version, and app version
  • Tips for Others: Share advice for others trying similar applications

Note: All submissions are reviewed before being featured or addressed. For use cases, we'll contact you if we'd like to showcase your application on our website. For bugs and feature requests, we'll prioritize based on impact and feasibility.
